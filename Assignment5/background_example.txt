flouting the rules and regulations of society. My specialty is Blackmail.

Skill Proficiencies: Deception, Stealth
Tool Proficiencies: One type of gaming set, thieves' tools

how to get messages to and from my contact, even over great distances; specifically, I know the local messengers,
corrupt caravan masters, and seedy sailors who can deliver messages for me.
