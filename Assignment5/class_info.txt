Info from player's handbook.

Barbarian
/*
A fierce warrior of primitive background who can enter a battle rage. You can later choose between the
Path of the Berserker, and the Path of the Totem Warrior.
Hit die: d12
Primary ability: Strength
Saving throw proficiencies: Strength, Constitution
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: Light and medium armor, shields, simple and martial weapons.
Skills: Choose two from Animal Handling, Athletics, Intimidation, Nature, Perception, and Survival.
Barbarian Features: Rage, Unarmored Defense
*/

Bard
/*
An inspiring magician whose power echoes the music of creation. You can later choose your Bard College:
The College of Lore, or the College of Valor.
Hit die: d8
Primary ability: Charisma
Saving throw proficiencies: Dexterity, Charisma
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: Light armor, simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords,
rapiers, shortswords.
Skills: Choose any three.
Bard Features: Spellcasting, Bardic Inspiration
*/

Cleric
/*
A priestly champion who wields divine magic in service of a higher power. Choose one domain related to
your deity: Knowledge, Life, Light, Nature, Tempest, Trickery, or War.
Hit die: d8
Primary ability: Wisdom
Saving throw proficiencies: Wisdom, Charisma
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: Light and medium armor, shields, simple weapons.
Skills: Choose two from History, Insight, Medicine, Persuasion, and Religion.
Cleric Features: Spellcasting, Divine Domain
.
Divine Domains:
Knowledge: Values learning and understanding above all. Learn two languages of your choice, and gain proficiency
in your choice of two of the following skills: Arcana, History, Nature, or Religion. Your proficiency bonus is
doubled for any ability check you make that uses either of those skills.
Life: Focuses on the vibrant positive energy that sustains all life. Gain proficiency with heavy armor, and your
healing spells are more effective.
Light: Promotes the ideals af rebirth and renewal, truth, vigilance, and beauty, often using the symbol of the sun.
Gain the light cantrip if you don't already know it, and you can interpose divine light between yourself and an
attacking enemy.
Nature: Learn one druid cantrip of your choice, and gain proficiency in one of the following skills of your choice:
Animal Handling, Nature, or Survival. Also gain proticiency with heavy armor.
Tempest: Gods of tempest govern storms, sea, and sky. Gain proficiency with martial weapons and heavy armor, and
you can thunderously rebuke attackers.
Trickery: Stands as a constant challenge to the accepted order among both gods and mortals. You can use your action
to touch a willing creature other than yourself to give it advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
War: Battle-focused, inspiring others to fight the good fight or offering acts of violence as prayers. Gain proficiency
with martial weapons and heavy armor. Your god delivers bolts of inspiration to you while you are engaged in battle.
*/

Druid
/*
A priest of the Old Faith, wielding the powers of nature — moonlight and plant growth,
fire and lightning — and adopting animal forms. You can later identify with a circle of druids:
the Circle of the Land or the Circle of the Moon.
Hit die: d8
Primary ability: Wisdom
Saving throw proficiencies: Intelligence, Wisdom
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: Light and medium armor (nonmetal), shields (nonmetal),
clubs, daggers, darts, javelins, maces, quarterstaffs, scimitars, sickles, slings, spears.
Skills: Choose two from Arcana, Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Religion, and Survival.
Druid Features: Druidic, Spellcasting
*/

Fighter
/*
A master of martial combat, skilled with a variety of weapons and armor. You can later choose an
archetype that you strive to emulate in your combat styles and techniques, being either Champion,
Battle Master, or Eldritch Knight.
Hit die: d10
Primary ability: Strength or Dexterity
Saving throw proficiencies: Strength, Constitution
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: All armor, shields, simple and martial weapons.
Skills: Choose two skills from Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, History, Insight, Intimidation, Perception, and Survival.
Fighter Features: Fighting Style, Second Wind
*/

Monk
/*
A master of martial arts, harnessing the power of the body in pursuit of physical and
spiritual perfection. You can later commit yourself to a monastic tradition: the Way of the Open
Hand, the Way of Shadow, or the Way of the Four Elements.
Hit die: d8
Primary ability: Dexterity & Wisdom
Saving throw proficiencies: Strength, Dexterity
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: Simple weapons, shortswords.
Skills: Choose two from Acrobatics, Athletics, History, Insight, Religion, and Stealth.
Monk Features: Unarmored Defense, Martial Arts
*/

Paladin
/*
A holy warrior bound to a sacred oath. Later on you can swear the oath that binds you as a paladin forever, 
choosing the Oath of Devotion, the Oath of the Ancients, ar the Oath of Vengeance.
Hit die: d10
Primary ability: Strength & Charisma
Saving throw proficiencies: Wisdom, Charisma
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: All armor, shields, simple and martial weapons.
Skills: Choose two from Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Persuasion, and Religion.
Paladin Features: Divine Sense, Lay on Hands
*/

Ranger
/*
A warrior who uses martial prowess and nature magic to combat threats on the edges
of civilization. You can later choose to emulate the ideals and	training of	a ranger conclave:
the Beast Conclave, the Hunter Conclave, or the Stalker Conclave.
Hit die: d10
Primary ability: Dexterity & Wisdom
Saving throw proficiencies: Strength, Dexterity
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: Light and medium armor, shields, simple and martial weapons.
Skills: Choose three from Animal Handling, Athletics, Insight, Investigation, Nature, Perception, Stealth, and Survival.
Ranger Features: Favored Enemy,	Natural	Explorer
*/

Rogue
/*
A scoundrel who uses stealth and trickery to overcome obstacles and enemies. Later on you can choose
an archetype that you emulate in the exercise of your rogue abilities: Thief, Assassin, or Arcane Trickster.
Hit die: d8
Primary ability: Dexterity
Saving throw proficiencies: Dexterity, Intelligence
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: Light armor, simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords,
rapiers, shortswords.
Skills: Choose four from Acrobatics, Athletics, Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Investigation, Perception,
Performance, Persuasion, Sleight of Hand, and Stealth.
Rogue Features: Expertise, Sneak Attack, Thieves' Cant
*/

Sorcerer
/*
Spellcaster who draws on inherent magic from a gift or bloodline. Choose a sorcerous origin, which describes the
source of your innate magical power: Draconic Bloodline or Wild Magic.
Hit die: d6
Primary ability: Charisma
Saving throw proficiencies: Constitution, Charisma
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows.
Skills: Choose two from Arcana, Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Persuasion, and Religion.
Sorcerer Features: Spellcasting, Sorcerous Origin
.
Sorcerous Origins:
Draconic Bloodline: Your innate magic comes from draconic magic that was mingled with your blood or that of your ancestors.
You choose one type of dragon as your ancestor. At 1st level, your hit point maximum increases by 1 and increases by 1 again
whenever you gain a level in this c1ass. Additionally, parts of your skin are covered by a thin sheen of dragon-like scales.
When you aren't wearing armor, your AC equals 13 + your Dexterity modifier.
Wild Magic: Your innate magic comes from the wild forces of chaos that underlie the order of creation. Your spellcasting can
unleash surges of untamed magic, and you can manipulate the forces of chance and chaos to gain advantage on one attack roll,
ability check, or saving throw.
*/

Warlock
/*
A wielder of magic that is derived from a bargain with an extraplanar entity. You have struck a bargain with an
otherworldly being of your choice: the Archfey, the Fiend, or the Great Old One.
Hit die: d8
Primary ability: Charisma
Saving throw proficiencies: Wisdom, Charisma
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: Light armor, simple weapons.
Skills: Choose two skills from Arcana, Deception, History, intimidation, Investigalion, Nature, and Religion.
Warlock Features: Otherworldly Patron, Pact Magic
.
Otherworldly Patron:
Archfey: Your patron is a lord or lady of the fey, a creature of legend who holds secrets that were forgotten before the
mortal races were born. Your patron bestows upon you the ability to project the beguiling and fearsome presence of the fey.
The Fiend: You have made a pact with a fiend from the lower planes of existence, a being whose aims are evil, even if you
strive against those aims. If you reduce a hostile creature to O hit points, you gain temporary hit points equal to your
Charisma modifier + your warlock level.
The Great Old One: Your patron is a mysterious entity whose nature is utterly foreign to the fabric of reality. You can communicate
telepathically with any creature you can see within 30 feet of you.
*/

Wizard
/*
A scholarly magic-user capable of manipulating the structures of reality. You can later choose an arcane tradition,
shaping your practice of magic through one of eight schools: Abjuration, Conjuration, Oivination, Enchantment,
Evocation, lllusion, Necromancy, or Transmutation.
Hit die: d6
Primary ability: Intelligence
Saving throw proficiencies: Intelligence, Wisdom
Armor and Weapon Proficiencies: Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows.
Skills: Choose two from Arcana, History, Insight, Investigation, Medicine, and Religion.
Wizard Features: Spellcasting, Arcane Recovery
*/
